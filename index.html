Case Study: Cyclistic

Keywords: R; Tableau; Dplyr

A capstone project for the Google Data Analytics Specialization certificate. The objective of this project is to understand the differences between casual (non-subscription) riders and annual members in order to design effective marketing strategies that convert casual riders into loyal annual members. By analyzing the Cyclistic historical bike trip data, we aim to uncover insights into user behavior, preferences, and patterns, and leverage these insights to develop targeted marketing tactics.
